# pizza-sales-analysis-SQL
SQL based data analysis project to uncover business insights from pizza sales data, revenue trends,customer behavior,and product performance insights.Includes advanced queries, business insights, and data- driven recommendations for optimizing sales and operations.
# (Problem statement)
The business aims to understand sales performance,identity top-selling products,and optimize operations.
> Which pizza generate the most revenue ?
> what are the peak ordering time?
>  Which products should be promoted or optimized ?
# Tools used 
SQL
# Key insights
> Identify the revenue insights where a small group of pizzas contributes a significiant share of total revenue.
> And also get premium pizzas generate higher revenue despite lower order volume.
> Identified customer behavior, peak ordering hours (12-2)PM and dinner(7-9)PM.
> Get the product prformance, largest-sized pizzas are the most frequently ordered.
# Business Recommendations
> Promote high- revenue pizzas through targeted marketing.
> Optimize staffing during peak hours to reduce wait time.
> Improve or remove low-performing menu items.
> Introduce offers during low- demanded periods.
